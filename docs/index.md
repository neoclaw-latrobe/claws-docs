# Hosted Claws Documentation

Welcome to the documentation for **Hosted Claws** — managed OpenClaw hosting with total privacy, enterprise-grade infrastructure, and AI models included.

## What is Hosted Claws?

Hosted Claws gives you your own private AI assistant powered by [OpenClaw](https://openclaw.ai). We handle all the infrastructure — you just connect your messaging apps and start working.

- **Your own instance** — no shared tenancy, no data mixing
- **AI models included** — from GPT-OSS to Claude, depending on your plan
- **Connect anywhere** — Discord, Telegram, Slack, WhatsApp, and more
- **Persistent memory** — your assistant remembers context between sessions
