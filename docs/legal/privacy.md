# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 27 February 2026*

## What We Collect

When you sign up, we collect:

- Your **email address** (from Stripe, for account management and support)
- Your **payment information** (handled entirely by Stripe — we never see your card details)

When you use the service, your OpenClaw instance stores:

- Your **workspace files** (notes, memory, agent configuration)
- Your **conversation history** with your assistant
- Any **data you choose to provide** to your assistant (emails, calendar info, etc.)

## Where Your Data Lives

- Your workspace is stored on **DigitalOcean Spaces** (Sydney, Australia)
- Your OpenClaw container runs on **DigitalOcean App Platform**
- AI model requests are processed by **DigitalOcean Gradient** (their AI inference platform)

Your data may pass through infrastructure in regions outside Australia as part of normal operation.

## What We Do With Your Data

Nothing, besides running your service.

- We **don't sell** your data
- We **don't share** your data with third parties (beyond our infrastructure providers)
- We **don't train** AI models on your data
- We **don't access** your workspace unless you ask us to for support purposes

## What Our Providers Do

Your prompts and AI responses are processed by third-party model providers through DigitalOcean. Their data handling practices are governed by their own privacy policies, not ours. We recommend reviewing [DigitalOcean's privacy policy](https://www.digitalocean.com/legal/privacy-policy).

## Data Retention

- While your subscription is active: all data is retained
- After cancellation: workspace data is kept for **30 days**, then permanently deleted
- Payment records: retained as required by law (managed by Stripe)

## Your Rights

You can at any time:

- **Export** your workspace data (ask your assistant or email us)
- **Delete** your account and all associated data (email us)
- **Ask** what data we hold about you (email us)

## Security

Each customer gets an isolated container. We don't share infrastructure between customers at the application level. Workspace data is synced to object storage but is **not encrypted at rest** beyond what DigitalOcean provides by default.

We do our best, but no system is perfectly secure. See our [Terms of Service](terms.md) for liability limitations.
