# Quick Start

Get your AI assistant running in under 5 minutes.

## 1. Choose a Plan

Visit [hosted-claws.com](https://hosted-claws.com) and select a plan:

| Plan | Price | Models | Storage |
|------|-------|--------|---------|
| **Standard** | $25/mo | GPT-OSS 20B & 120B, GPT-5 nano | 100MB |
| **Pro** | $50/mo | + GPT-5 mini, Llama 3.3 70B | 500MB |
| **Enterprise** | $200/mo | + GPT-5.2, Codex, Claude Sonnet & Opus | 2GB |

## 2. Check Your Email

Within 15 minutes of purchase, you'll receive an onboarding email with:

- Your **secure URL** (e.g. `https://yourname.hosted-claws.com`)
- Your **access token** (included in the link)

## 3. Open Your Assistant

Click the link in your email. You'll land on your assistant's web interface — a one-time setup page.

## 4. Connect a Messaging App

Your assistant will walk you through connecting your first channel. Supported platforms:

- **Discord** — Add a bot to your server
- **Telegram** — Create a bot via BotFather
- **Slack** — Install the OpenClaw app
- **WhatsApp** — Scan a QR code
- **Signal** — Link as a secondary device

!!! tip
    Once you've connected two channels, the web interface is automatically disabled for security. You can always re-enable it by messaging your assistant.

## 5. Start Chatting

Send a message to your assistant on your connected platform. It's ready to help — ask it anything, give it tasks, or start building your workflow.
