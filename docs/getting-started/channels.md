# Connecting Channels

Your OpenClaw assistant can connect to multiple messaging platforms. Here's how to set up each one.

## Discord

1. Tell your assistant: *"Set up Discord"*
2. It will create a bot and give you an invite link
3. Add the bot to your Discord server
4. Start messaging in any channel the bot can see

## Telegram

1. Tell your assistant: *"Set up Telegram"*
2. It will guide you through creating a bot via [@BotFather](https://t.me/BotFather)
3. Send your bot token to the assistant
4. Message your new Telegram bot

## Slack

1. Tell your assistant: *"Set up Slack"*
2. Follow the OAuth flow to install the app in your workspace
3. Invite the bot to channels where you want it active

## WhatsApp

1. Tell your assistant: *"Set up WhatsApp"*
2. A QR code will appear — scan it with WhatsApp on your phone
3. Your assistant is now available in WhatsApp

## Signal

1. Tell your assistant: *"Set up Signal"*
2. Follow the instructions to link as a secondary device
3. Your assistant is now available in Signal

---

!!! info "Security Note"
    After connecting two channels, the web interface is automatically disabled. All future interaction happens through your connected messaging apps. You can re-enable the web UI at any time by asking your assistant.
