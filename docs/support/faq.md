# FAQ

## General

**What is Hosted Claws?**
Hosted Claws is a managed hosting service for [OpenClaw](https://openclaw.ai) — an open-source AI assistant framework. We run your own private AI assistant on our infrastructure so you don't have to.

**What can my assistant do?**
Anything OpenClaw can do: answer questions, manage tasks, send emails, search the web, write code, manage files, set reminders, and much more. It learns your preferences over time through its persistent memory.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Each customer gets their own isolated container. We don't share data between customers, and we don't train on your data.

## Technical

**What happens when the container restarts?**
Your workspace is automatically synced to persistent storage. On restart, it's pulled back. You won't lose your data.

**Can I SSH into my instance?**
No. Containers are locked down for security. You interact with your assistant through messaging channels only.

**Can I install custom skills?**
Yes — your assistant can install OpenClaw skills from [Claw Hub](https://clawhub.com) through natural language commands.

**What models can I use?**
Depends on your tier. See [Available Models](../models/index.md) for the full list.
